The sharp, geometric, sans serif is prominently insipired by the modern and brutalist architecture through the bay area. The "a" is entirely based off the structure of the De Young Museum in Golden Gate Park, San Fransisco. This font features sharp angles and contrasting smooth curves. The letterform is based off two circles forming the upper and lowercase "O"s.
