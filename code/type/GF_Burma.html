This ornamental serif font is insipred by nature from Big Sur, local gardens and parks, and the Victorian styled homes through Pacific Grove and San Fransisco. It is a cross between transitional and old styled serif fonts. Highlighted by its sharp, piercing descenders and a diagonal stress on the bowls and serifs. There is a low contrast between the thick and thin lines. It also fetures a double story "g" and "a".

Comes in Dispaly and normal - for body text use.
